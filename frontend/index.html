Why Join?

Hackathon 9.0 provides an opportunity for participants to build real technology prototypes in a competitive and collaborative environment. Participants will get hands-on experience, direct input from mentors and judges, as well as opportunities to expand networking in the field of technology. In addition to strengthening the project portfolio, participants also have the opportunity to win prizes and official certificates.

FAQ

Registration is done through the Register page on the website. Teams need to create a group account, fill in the leader and member data, then upload the required documents before the registration deadline.

Each team consists of a minimum of 3 people and a maximum of 5 people. All members must be officially registered and part of the same team from the beginning of the competition.

Participants are free to use any tools, frameworks, and technologies as long as they do not violate the rules. All developed works must be original and created during the duration of the hackathon.

Timeline

Open Registration: 1 May 2025

The deadline for registration of participants is May 31, 2025. Make sure all data and documents are complete before that date.

Close Registration: 25 May 2025

Registration will be closed on the date that has been set. Teams that have not completed their data or documents after the deadline will not be processed to the next selection stage.

Technical Meeting: 28 May 2025

Technical meeting is conducted online to explain the rules of the competition, submission format, evaluation criteria, and Q&A session with the organizers.

Competition Day: 1-2 June 2025

Competition day is the main session for development and project presentation. Each team will work on their solution and present it in front of mentors and judges.
